Privacy Policy

ScreenSense - AI Screen Coach

Effective Date: March 1, 2026

ScreenSense is built with privacy as its core principle. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data. Everything happens on your device.

1. Our Privacy Philosophy

ScreenSense was designed from the ground up to be completely private. We believe your screen time data is deeply personal, and no company should have access to it - including us.

We literally cannot see your data, even if we wanted to. There are no servers to store it on, no APIs to transmit it through, and no accounts to link it to.

2. Data We Access

ScreenSense accesses the following data, all of which remains exclusively on your device:

  • Screen Time Data: Which apps you use and for how long, accessed through Apple's Screen Time API (DeviceActivity framework). We see app names and usage durations, NOT the content within those apps.
  • Mood Entries: If you choose to log your mood, these entries are stored locally on your device using SwiftData.
  • Goals and Preferences: Your custom goals, settings, and preferences are stored locally in the app's sandbox.

3. Data We Do NOT Collect

We want to be absolutely clear about what we do NOT do:

  • We do NOT collect any personal information
  • We do NOT require an account or sign-up
  • We do NOT transmit any data to external servers
  • We do NOT use any analytics SDKs (no Firebase, no Mixpanel, no Google Analytics, nothing)
  • We do NOT track your location
  • We do NOT access your contacts, photos, or messages
  • We do NOT read the content within your apps (we only see which app is open and for how long)
  • We do NOT use any advertising frameworks or ad tracking
  • We do NOT share any data with third parties
  • We do NOT use cookies or web tracking of any kind

4. On-Device Processing

All data analysis, AI classification, and insight generation happens entirely on your iPhone using Apple's Core ML framework. No data ever leaves your device for processing.

  • AI Models: Our classification models are bundled within the app and run locally using Core ML.
  • Data Storage: All your data is stored in the app's sandboxed storage using Apple's SwiftData framework.
  • No Network Requests: The app works entirely offline. It makes zero network requests for data processing or analytics.

5. Third-Party Services

ScreenSense uses the following Apple services only:

  • Apple StoreKit 2: For processing the one-time Pro purchase. Apple handles the transaction; we receive only a purchase confirmation. We do not see your payment details.
  • Apple Screen Time API: For accessing your device usage data. This data is provided by the operating system and never transmitted to us.

We do not use any third-party SDKs, libraries, or services beyond Apple's own frameworks.

6. Data Storage and Security

  • All data is stored in the app's iOS sandbox, protected by Apple's built-in security mechanisms.
  • Data is encrypted at rest by iOS when your device has a passcode enabled.
  • The app does not create backups to external services.
  • If iCloud sync is enabled by you, data syncs only between YOUR devices through YOUR iCloud account.

7. Data Deletion

You have complete control over your data:

  • Delete All Data: Use the "Delete All Data" option in Settings to permanently erase all app data from your device.
  • Uninstall: Deleting the app removes all data permanently. Since no data exists on our servers, deletion is complete and irreversible.

8. Children's Privacy

ScreenSense does not knowingly collect data from children under the age of 13. The app does not collect data from any users of any age. Parents may use ScreenSense to understand their own screen time habits.

9. In-App Purchases

ScreenSense offers a one-time Pro purchase ($4.99) processed entirely through Apple's App Store. We do not collect, store, or have access to any payment information. All transactions are handled by Apple.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app and on this page with an updated effective date. Our core commitment to privacy will never change: your data stays on your device.

11. Your Rights

Since we do not collect any personal data, traditional data rights (access, correction, deletion, portability) are inherently fulfilled - your data is already entirely in your possession on your device.
