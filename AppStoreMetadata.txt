APP PRIVACY (App Store Privacy Labels):

Data NOT Collected:
ScreenSense does not collect any data. All processing happens
on-device. There are no servers, no analytics, no tracking.

Specifically:
- No contact info collected
- No health & fitness data sent anywhere
- No usage data collected by us
- No diagnostics collected
- No identifiers collected

- No location data collected

transmitted anywhere.
